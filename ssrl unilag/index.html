Welcome to the SSRL

The Social Sciences Research Laboratories (SSRL) is a unique and leading network of 10 research laboratories
made possible by the combined support of the Nigeria Foundation for Innovation,
the Government of Lagos and the University of Lagos.

As a research support unit, the SSRL assists faculty, staff, students and community-based organizations undertaking research in the social sciences by providing access to specialized research infrastructure (computers, equipment and software) and research space (specific and multi-purpose),
and providing access to research supports in the form of specialists with backgrounds and training in specific social science research methodologies (quantitative and survey research; qualitative research; experimental research;
GIS and cartography; social network analysis; among many others).

Our Mission:

To provide shared research infrastructure and technical and administrative support to faculty, staff and students at the University of Saskatchewan and beyond, to facilitate the design, delivery and dissemination of cutting-edge social science research.

We invite you to explore our website to learn more about who we are, what we do and the various facilities and services available throughout the SSRL and our 10 research laboratories. Alternatively, you can view,
download or print our SSRL brochure that provides a comprehensive inventory of our available facilities and services.

To learn more about us or to initiate a project, please contact us!
